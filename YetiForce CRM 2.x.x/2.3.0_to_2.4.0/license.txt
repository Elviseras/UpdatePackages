This update package should not be executed in the production version of the CRM!
Required PHP version needs to be equal or greater than 5.4.
During the update process some of the configuration files, such as .htaccess, performance.php, developer.php, will be overwritten.
The following modules together with data will be removed: Invoices, Sales Order, Opportunities, Calculations, Requirement Cards, Quotes enquires, Purchase Order, Quotes, Costs
Attention: You will be redirected to Home page when update is completed.
